David Suzuki

Main Page / Acomplishments / Gallery

1963: Worked as a university professor in the University of British Columbia (UBC)
1974: Hosted a programe called "Quirks and Quarks" on CBC AM radio. The show interviewed scientists and answerd viewers questions.
1979: Started hosting the television series on CBC, The Nature of Things. The program intends to draw attention to the threats faced by winglife, and the bbenefits of renewable energy. This show still airs has episodes today.
1991: Co-authored with Anita Gordon to write "It's a Matter of Survival." The book prediced the course of humanity in 50 years and how to improve it.
1997: Produced the documentary "Yellowstone to Yukon: The Wildlands Project" which looked at David Foreman's project about creating buffer zones around wild reserves to preserve biological diversity.
